# How GammaRemover Works

Gamma exports may include visible branding in PDF or PowerPoint files. GammaRemover is designed to clean supported visible branding elements from standard Gamma exports where those elements are stored in a removable form.

Use GammaRemover here: https://gammaremover.com/

## PDF and PPTX cleanup are different

PDF files and PowerPoint files store content in different ways.

A PDF export may contain:

* Text objects
* Images
* Links
* Annotations
* Positioned visual elements
* Page-level drawing instructions

A PPTX export may contain:

* Slide objects
* Shapes
* Text boxes
* Master/layout elements
* Images
* Linked objects

Because the structures are different, PDF cleanup and PPTX cleanup use different approaches.

## When cleanup works best

GammaRemover works best when the visible branding is stored as a separate removable element, such as a text object, link, annotation, shape, or positioned object.

## When cleanup may be limited

Cleanup may be limited when branding is flattened into an image or merged directly into the page background.

In those cases, removing the branding without changing the image may not be possible.

## Keep original files

Always keep a copy of your original Gamma export before processing any file.
